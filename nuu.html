ʻUkulele!

ʻUkulele is a 4-8 string instrument that you can play! You can play any song that you want.
I prefer strumming but you can fingerstyle.ʻUkulele is the most famous Hawaiian instrument. The ʻukulele can be played really fast or REALLY SLOW .The 4 most famous ʻukulele brands in Hawaiʻi are Kala, Lanikai,Kamaka, and Kohala. Different types of wood are used for ʻukulele.
